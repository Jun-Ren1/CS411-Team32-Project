BMI Categories

Underweight = <18.5

Normal weight = 18.5–24.9

Overweight = 25–29.9

Obesity = 30 or greater

Weight Management Tips

For Weight Loss

Method Description Notes
Controlled Diet Ensure calorie intake is less than calorie expenditure. Avoid high-calorie foods. Monitor portion sizes.
Increased Exercise Engage in at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic exercise per week. Include activities like brisk walking, jogging, or swimming.
Adequate Hydration Drink enough water to control appetite and maintain hydration. Consider water-rich foods like fruits and vegetables.

For Weight Gain

Method Description Notes
Increased Caloric Intake Consume high-calorie but healthy foods like nuts, avocados, and olive oil. Focus on nutrient-dense options.
High Protein Diet Ensure sufficient protein intake to promote muscle growth. Include chicken breast, fish, and legumes. Consult with a nutritionist for personalized recommendations.
Muscle Training Engage in weight training exercises to build muscle while gaining weight. Work with a fitness professional for proper guidance.
